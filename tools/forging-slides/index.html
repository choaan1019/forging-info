Weaknesses

Opportunities
  • Electric assist & electronic control — emerging growth areas
  • Eco-friendly materials & low-carbon production gaining traction
  • Rapidly growing demand in China & India for high-quality components
Threats
  • Chinese & SE Asian manufacturers challenging on cost
  • Geopolitical changes disrupting material supply & trade
  • Global inflation suppressing demand for high-end components
